FAQ

Frequently Ask Questions

Let us answer your questions, please feel free to connect and ask more on our Discord.

What is Stray Cat Tribe?

Stray Cat Tribe, crafted by On Off Games, is an exhilarating web3 project featuring an array of game modes and mini-games. Our primary focus is to unite Web3 players and create captivating experiences across different platforms such as computers, consoles, and handheld devices. Our goal is to capture the interest of players from all walks of gaming life.

What are the core gameplay mechanics of Stray Cat Tribe?

Stray Cat Tribe will feature various game modes, and we are working on making these game modes playable on computers, consoles, and handheld devices. Each mode will have unique mechanics, and the mini-games, with their diverse mechanics, will be playable on the website. We share information about the game modes and mini-games on our social media accounts, so stay tuned for updates on what these games entail. Within the main game, numerous party game mechanics enhance the gaming experience. One significant advantage of the game is the ability to play alongside your web2 player friends, allowing you to earn rewards. We aim to facilitate gameplay for up to four players on a single device with the support of a single wallet. Stay connected for more details on the exciting features we have in store.

What is the token of Stray Cat Tribe, and how is it used?

Stray Cat Tribe's $SCT token adds a gamified layer to our experience. Players can leverage tokens for exclusive content access, rewards, and active participation within our gaming ecosystem.

How can I join Stray Cat Tribe?

To join Stray Cat Tribe, visit the official website or follow social media accounts. Additionally, use communication channels like Discord to engage with the community and stay informed about updates.

What is the release date of the game?

There is currently no set release date. As Stray Cat Tribe is in the development phase, staying connected with the community and following official announcements will provide up-to-date information.

What unique features does Stray Cat Tribe offer?

One of the key features that Stray Cat Tribe offers in its gaming experience is the almost unrestricted access to the game from nearly any platform you desire. It stands out as the first game to seamlessly integrate Split Screen Web3 functionality. Whether playing with friends or enjoying some solo time, the game accommodates up to four players on a single computer or console, providing a fun, humorous, and enjoyable experience. Whether meeting new friends in the game, playing together, or engaging in solo challenges, various rewards await you.
